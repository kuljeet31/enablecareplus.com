At Enable Care Plus Pty Ltd, we are committed to delivering high-quality, person-centred supports aligned with NDIS Practice Standards. Our goal is to empower participants to live independently, build skills, and actively participate in their communities.

Assist Access / Maintain Employment :

We support participants in preparing for, finding, and maintaining employment. Our services include skill development, workplace readiness, and ongoing support to help individuals achieve sustainable employment outcomes.

Assist-Life Stage, Transitiont :

We provide guidance and practical assistance during major life transitions. Whether moving to independent living, changing services, or planning future goals, we ensure a smooth and confident transition.

Assist-Personal Activities :

Our team supports daily personal care needs including hygiene, mobility, dressing, and other essential activities to promote dignity, independence, and wellbeing.

Assist-Travel / Transport :

We help participants access their community safely by providing travel and transport support for appointments, work, education, and social activities.

Community Nursing Care :

Qualified nursing professionals provide clinical care and health-related supports tailored to individual medical needs, ensuring safety and quality care at all times.

Daily Tasks / Shared Living :

We assist participants in shared or independent living arrangements with daily household routines, meal preparation, and maintaining a comfortable home environment.

Innovative Community Participation :

Our programs encourage participants to build confidence, develop social skills, and engage in meaningful community activities.

Development-Life Skills :

We focus on building essential life skills such as budgeting, communication, cooking, decision-making, and independent living capabilities.

Household Tasks :

Support with cleaning, laundry, gardening, and general household maintenance to maintain a safe and healthy living environment.

Participate Community :

We encourage active community involvement through social events, recreational activities, and group programs that enhance inclusion and connection.

Group / Centre Activities :

Structured group programs designed to promote social interaction, skill-building, and personal development in a supportive environment.
